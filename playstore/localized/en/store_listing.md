# TodayPlay

## Short Description
Generate a route quest for couples, friends, or families in 30 seconds.

## Full Description
TodayPlay turns relationship needs, city places, route planning, interaction tasks, map jumps, check-ins, and reward points into an executable route card. Choose who you are going with, the city, time, budget, transport, and mood, then receive a route quest with stops, reasons, estimated cost, photo ideas, risk tips, interaction tasks, and a completion card.

This version uses curated local and mock data only. It does not scrape Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Meituan, Amap, or other third-party platforms, does not show unauthorized images, and does not store API keys in the app. Map entry points open external map apps or a browser through system actions.
