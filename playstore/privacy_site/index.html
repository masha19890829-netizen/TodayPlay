English

TodayPlay helps users create route quests for couples, friends, families, or solo trips. It uses relationship type, city, time, budget, transportation mode, and preferences to generate itinerary cards, check-in tasks, local reward points, completion cards, and history.

  • Current data is mainly stored locally on the device: route preferences, generated history, task status, check-in status, local points, and feedback.
  • Users can clear local route history inside the app through Privacy & Local Data -> Clear local history.
  • The app does not request precise location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, call logs, health data, or face recognition permissions.
  • Map buttons open external map apps or browser maps with destination information. The app does not force arrival verification.
  • Google Play Billing may provide product IDs and purchase tokens. Durable paid benefits must be granted only after backend verification.
  • POI and social inspiration content in the current version is mock or curated sample data. The app does not scrape third-party platforms or display unauthorized third-party images.
